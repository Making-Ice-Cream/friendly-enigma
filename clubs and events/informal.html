Description

1. Each team can consists of maximum 5 members.

Shake and Dance


Description
This will be a hide and seek type fun event where answer of basic IQ level questions will have to be revealed by participants by the help of concealed clues given by organisers .The team who seek the answer at an early stage of time than others , will be given more points as compared to other teams.

1. Maximum 4 members can be in 1 team
2. Mobile phone is not allowed.
3. Decision of organisers will be final.Team which will unnecessary argue with organisers will be disqualified after first warning.
