Solo Face-Off

Rules:

1.There are three rounds for this competition as listed below:
Elimination round: Contestants will have to perform for a time duration of 1 min on the track of their own choice.
Semi final round: A face-off round, your opponent will be selected by the judges randomly and you have to perform on a random track.
Final Round: The winners of the previous round will battle each other for winning the title, again a face-off round.
2) The track of semi-finals and final round will be played on the spot.
3) Participants can incorporate styles like Hiphop, Tutting, Flooring, Breaking or B-Boying, Krumping, Popping, Locking, Bollywood and freestyle. Styles other than these are not encouraged.
4) Usage of props is allowed. These have to be brought by contestants themselves.
5) Track should be brought in a pen drive in .mp3 format and must be submitted 15-30 mins before the competition.

7) Every team should have their id cards and photocopy of bank passbook
Judging Criteria:
1)Participants shall be penalized for not adhering to the time limits.
2) Variety in the moves shown.
3) Clarity, Perfection, Difficulty level and Entertainment Quotient of the moves shown.
