Hallabol – Nukkad Natak

Rules:

● Maximum no. of participants allowed per team is 25 (including the team members on music).
● Time limit- maximum time limit is 30 minutes (from entry to exit).
● Exceeding the maximum time limit will lead to deduction of points.
Theme language of the street play should be in Hindi, English or Punjabi and it can have excerpts of other language too.
● Any prop or music instruments won’t be provided by the organizers.
● Teams are not allowed to make any noise when the other team is performing, else points will be deducted.
● The soft copy of the script has to be sent to the organizers 10 days before at dramaticsnitj@gmail.com . The final participants will be selected on the basis of script.
● Recorded music is not allowed.
● Only percussion or membrane instruments are allowed.
● Plagiarism is strictly prohibited. The script should be original.
● Vulgarity or offensive gestures or remarks are strictly not allowed.
● Please note that dialogues, acting, script, theme, each element has certain points.
● Every team should have their id cards and photocopy of bank passbook

Judging Criteria:
Screenplay, Script, Voice Modulation, Concept/Theme and Acting.

Monoact

Rules:

● Each participant has to prepare a mono-act of maximum of 8 minutes (exceeding time will carry negative marks.)
● Mono-act can be mimicry, mime, any monologue or stand-up comedy.
● Except mimicry Mon-act should be original.
● Any kind of external help or prompts is not allowed.
● The act should be cultured and a pleasant piece of art. Vulgarity or any type of offensive gestures or remarks may lead to disqualification.
For background score only .mp3 format is supported.
● Every team should have their id cards and photocopy of bank passbook

Judging Criteria:
Acting, spontaneity, script and overall impact.

Venue: SAC
Time: 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM

Dumb Charades

Rules:

● Maximum no. of participants allowed per team is 4.
● No of rounds : 5(Theme of each round will be different e.g. movies, personalities)
● A member of the Team will be given a word or phrase depending upon the theme (e.g. movies, personalities) of each round , identified member from Team has to enact the film name or the song via his body language without any lip movement to the remaining members of his team.
● Others in his team have to guess the name of the movie or song by following the gestures.
● Maximum time allocated to the each team to enact and find out the assigned word or phrase will 90s.
● Every team should have their id cards and photocopy of bank passbook

Date: 21st March
Venue: SAC
Time: 12:00 PM to 2:00PM

Vidhushak- Comedy Stage Play

Rules:

● Play could be in English, Hindi or Bi-lingual, or a Musical Drama.
● The maximum team size allowed is 18 members (including one light in- charge, one sound in-charge and backstage members).
●Maximum 15 members are allowed on stage. Two teams from same college can participate.
● The performance should be an original play . There are constraints regarding that. Inappropriate content and vulgarity/obscenity will NOT be tolerated and will lead to immediate disqualification.
● Use of flammable substances like candles, matches or cigarettes or any alcoholic product during performance on stage is not allowed and will result in disqualification.
● Participants must bring the background music for the play in a standard secondary storage device.
● In any case judge’s decision will be final.
● All teams must submit a list of required props.
● Time Limit: 30 minutes to perform the play (empty stage to empty stage). Not adhering to time limit will result in negative marking.
● There should be a sense of political/socail/contemporary satire from script of play with accent of comedy.
● Every team should have their id cards and photocopy of bank passbook

Judging Criteria:
Screenplay, Script, Voice Modulation, Concept/Theme and Acting


Date: 20th March
Venue: CSH
Time: 9:00 AM to 11:30 AM
