NIT J’s Got Talent

Rules:

1. This is a mega event of NITJ Movie Club.
2. This is a two round event in which Round 1 will be online. Participants will submit a video which shows their talent.
Round 2 will be on stage in which participants will show their talent in front of judges and audience live.
Team Size: At most 5 in the core team (No particular limit for supports)
Rounds:
1 (Online) Time limit for video is max 1 min. This round is for elimination.
2(Live) Judging will be based on best performance and talent.
Any type of talent is welcomed in this event such as dancing, singing, poetry, stand up,rubix cube solving etc.

Date: 21st March.
Venue: CSH
Time: 9:00AM to 11:00AM

“Save the World”(Short flim)

Rules:

This is a short film event which consists of two rounds. The short film must deliver the message "Humanity still exists" .
1. In round 1 teaser of the short film will be posted on instagram page of movie club.
2. Teaser should be less than 1 minute.
3. In round 2 participants have to submit their short film.
4. Short film should be less than 4 minutes.
5. No. of participants should be 3-5.
6. Judging Criteria
    i. originality of the content
    ii. video editing.
    iii. Cinematography
    iv. Clarity of short film.
    v. Sound effects.
    vi.Acting
    vii. Overall impacts.
7. Short film should not harm any religious beliefs and must not be discriminated on any basis.
8. Result will be absolute.


Date: 20th March
Venue: SB1
Time: 9:00AM to 10:30AM
